How to Prepare Sisig Tofu?

by: Ralph Ryan B. Rubilla

Ingredients:

  • 1L cooking oil(for frying)
  • 1/2 kg firm tofu, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 pc red/white onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 2 pc bird’s eye chili (Siling Labuyo)
  • 5 pcs fresh long green chili (Siling Pangsigang)
  • Liquid Seasoning (to taste)
  • 1/3 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 pcs calamansi
  • 2 pcs Eggs (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start by taking a shallow pan and filling it with cooking oil.
  2. Set the heat to high, and once small bubbles start to appear, proceed to deep-fry the tofu cubes until they turn brown and crisp.
  3. In another pan, heat it up over medium heat.
  4. Sauté onions and garlic in oil, then add the fried tofu and chilies.
  5. Adjust the flavor by incorporating Liquid Seasoning of your choice according to your preference.
  6. Turn off the heat then add the Mayonnaise, and mix thoroughly.
  7. To complete the dish, serve it with calamansi and optionally include a fried egg.
  8. Prepare a generous serving of rice, as you'll likely find yourself enjoying more than usual.

Enjoy Special Sisig Tofu together with your Family!
